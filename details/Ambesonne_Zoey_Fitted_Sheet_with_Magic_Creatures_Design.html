Ambesonne Zoey Fitted Sheet

Transform your child's bedroom into a magical space with the Ambesonne Zoey Fitted Sheet. This adorable bedding option features a stunning design of whimsical magic creatures and elegant calligraphic alphabet letters, creating a whimsical and imaginative atmosphere for bedtime.

Crafted from 100% brushed microfiber, this fitted sheet provides a luxuriously soft and durable surface that feels like a gentle embrace for your little one. Its silky smooth texture is perfect for ensuring a comfortable and restful sleep.

The all-round elastic deep pocket ensures a secure and snug fit on the mattress, preventing the sheet from coming loose during the night. This design is ideal for California King mattresses up to 16" in thickness, making it a versatile choice for any bedroom setup.
